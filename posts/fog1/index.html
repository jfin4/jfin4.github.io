Under the Weather, Part 1

Creating a High-resolution Fog Map Using MODIS Satellite Data

December 4, 2020

Summary: In this series we are exploring the relationship between fogginess and home prices in the city of Seaside, California, using remotely sensed data and online home price estimates. Part 1 describes accessing and processing MODIS satellite data to create a 250-meter resolution fog frequency map.

Had I been slightly more ambitious I would have doubled by sample size by downloading MYD35 as well.
